About Us

Located at the heart of Manchester. Brewology comes from an idea that everyone can enjoy coffee.


We specialise in hand roasted ethically sourced speciality coffees.


It doesn't matter if you like your coffee light or sweet, or you prefer strong coffee with no sugar, you can always enjoy it with your personal preferences in here. It's our pleasure to help you find the best crafted drink that suits your taste in the best way!

Our Coffee

Roasting coffee is both an art and a science. Every bean variety that arrives to our Roastery is put through rigorous testing and experimentation to find the perfect balance of flavours for us to use.


Our house blends are from South/Central America & Africa, each roasted and rested for 5 days before being mixed.


We regularly feature guest espresso from some of the best roaster in Europe and further afield.

Our Food

Using specialist diary, meat and vegetable producers to put together an amazing plate of food.


We constantly look for great suppliers who share our passion for flavour.
